// about

Small on purpose.

Straybits Corporation is a small, independent technology company based in Canada. We've spent decades building, securing, and untangling software systems — from scrappy startups to critical infrastructure.

We keep a short client list and take on new work sparingly, mostly by referral. The rest of the time we're off building things we find interesting. If you think we can help with something, we'd still love to hear from you.
